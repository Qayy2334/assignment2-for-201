Penang Laksa

Penang Betel Nut Noodles (or Penang Sour Noodle Soup) is a very popular local delicacy. Its characteristic lies in the sour and spicy soup base, which usually uses mackerel as the main ingredient, and adds herbs, chili, dried shrimps, etc. The taste is sour, spicy, fresh and fragrant, and it is very appetizing.
